## FAQ — 5 Questions

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial, no login.
The audit is free forever. We show you savings
before we ask for anything.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: SpendLens is built by Credex, which sources
discounted AI credits for startups. If your audit
shows significant savings, we may reach out about
how Credex can help capture even more.

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**
A: Every recommendation uses pricing verified
directly from official vendor pages. The audit
logic is hardcoded rules reviewed against real
billing — not AI guesswork.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Your email and company name are never shown
publicly. Shareable audit URLs show only tool
names and savings numbers — never your identity.

**Q: Which tools do you support?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT,
Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf.
More added regularly based on requests.
